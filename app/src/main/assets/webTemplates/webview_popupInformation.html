Presentation

Welcome to your news feeds that i've tried to be as simple as possible.
So, in this manual i'll only describe some essential things

It takes only few minutes to read this little starting manual

First, let me tell you how to configure your first RSS FEEDS

How to configure your first RSS FEEDS

1- Click on the icon tools sit on the top menu



2- Click on the add rss icon sit on the top menu



3- A new page open to configure your rss feed, follow the instructions witch are on the top of new page (you can scroll down within the page if necessary)

4- After addind at least one news (you can add more several folowwing), click to back button to return to the configuration screen

4- On the configutation screen click to the button "Refresh news list" at th bottom of the screen


How to configure your categories

You can put each of your rss feed in a category, application commes with some categories but you can configure them as you want.
You can : add, modify and delete category

1- Click on the icon tools sit on the top menu



2- Click on the add category icon sit on the top menu



3- A new page open to configure your category :
- On the top of the page you can select one category with the dropdown list and modify/delete it

- On the bottom of the page you can add one category after set a name for the new vategory

4- After addind/modifying or deleting category, click to back button to return to the configuration screen


Tools Bar



From the left to th right you have :

- To refresh the news list
- To open the user's manual page (this manual)
- To configure your RSS and categories
- 3 dots for open hidden menu (see below)

Hidden Menu

In this menu you can acces some features like :

- A little search engine to find news containing some words
- A buttton to return to home page (page with news list)
- A button to see all favorites news
- A button to see unreaded news
- A button to see saved news

Contextual menu for news

For each news you can access to a contextual menu with a long click on the right of the news (on the image to be more accurate)



You can :

- Mark news as readed
- Mark news as unreaded
- Add news to favorites
- Remove news from favorites
- Share news by email
- Save news permanently to your phone
